# Supported Hardware

The Drone Detector is designed exclusively for the **LILYGO T-Beam S3-Core** device.

## LILYGOÂ® T-Beam S3-Core

The T-Beam S3-Core is the recommended hardware platform for the Drone Detector project. It combines a powerful ESP32-S3 microcontroller with the Semtech SX1262 LoRa transceiver and GPS capabilities.

| Component | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| MCU | ESP32-S3 (WiFi & Bluetooth 5 LE) |
| Radio | Semtech SX1262 LoRa Transceiver |
| Frequencies | 433 MHz, 868 MHz, or 915 MHz (varies by model) |
| GPS | NEO-M10S or Quectel L76K |
| Interface | USB-C, Boot/Reset buttons |

### Why T-Beam S3-Core?

- **SX1262 Transceiver** - Modern radio with excellent sensitivity and frequency range
- **ESP32-S3** - Powerful dual-core processor with ample memory
- **Integrated GPS** - Log location of detected drone signals
- **Compact form factor** - Standalone core module or with Supreme daughterboard
- **Active community** - Well-documented and supported

### Variants

| Variant | Frequency Band | Region |
|---------|---------------|--------|
| 433 MHz | 433.0 - 434.8 MHz | EU, Asia |
| 868 MHz | 868.0 - 870.0 MHz | EU |
| 915 MHz | 902.0 - 928.0 MHz | US, Australia |

Choose the variant that matches your region and the drone frequencies you wish to detect.
